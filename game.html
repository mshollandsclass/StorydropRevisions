The Research

Why it works.

CenterStaged isn't built on intuition alone. Every design decision is rooted in how students actually learn — and what makes content stick.

🎮

Play drives retention

Game-based learning increases engagement and long-term recall. When students are playing, they're not thinking about learning — they're just doing it.

📖

Stories make words stick

Research shows vocabulary embedded in narrative context is retained far longer than definitions alone. StoryDrop is built on this principle.

🖐

Ownership builds confidence

When students create — not just consume — they take ownership. Creative freedom in writing is directly linked to motivation and writing performance.
